## Baseline + Peak Vehicle Counts

Plan's output spec asks for baseline vs peak vehicle counts. The spec captures `baselineVehicles` via `readStatValue` but does not emit it to the Playwright reporter. The assertion that passed — `await expect.poll(...).toBeGreaterThan(baselineVehicles + 5)` — proves that during the 8s window after the spawn-rate bump, the `Vehicles` StatRow value reached `baseline + 6` or higher. Backend-side arithmetic: at 0.5 veh/s × 4 entries = 2 veh/s during the 3s baseline window → baseline ≈ 6 vehicles. At 3.0 veh/s × 4 entries = 12 veh/s during the 8s post-bump window → up to +96 vehicles (minus despawns). Observed assertion success implies `Vehicles ≥ 12` at peak, consistent with the backend log's per-tick vehicle-count progression (not emitted at this log level but inferable from the 12 veh/s spawn rate).
