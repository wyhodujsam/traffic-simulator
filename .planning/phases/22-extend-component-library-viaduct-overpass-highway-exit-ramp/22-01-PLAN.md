<objective>
Add two new `ComponentSpec` records — `Viaduct` and `HighwayExitRamp` — following Phase 21 patterns
(`TIntersection` / `SignalFourWay` / `StraightSegment`). Extend the sealed `permits` clause and
cover the new records with unit tests. Strictly additive: no edits to existing records.

Purpose: Bring Phase 22 component catalogue up from 4 to 6 types so Claude can identify viaducts
and highway exit ramps in OSM imagery.

Output: Two new record files, one updated sealed interface, two new expansion test files, and
extensions to the existing `ReverseRoadIdComponentTest` that assert the `_in`/`_out` contract
survives for the new components.
</objective>
